Hello and welcome to my Stock Stalker Website!

Below you will find a brief description of what this application can do, what it cannot do and how to use it. You can also find build notes and credits below.
General Purpose

The Stock Stalker application helps you keep track of and perform pattern analysis on stocks from exchanges around the world. At core it is a very simple paper trading application.

Functionalities

With the application you can

  • Create and maintain multiple Portfolios
  • Add Stocks to your Portfolios
  • Monitor the changing of the daily prices of your stocks
  • Perform pattern analysis on the stocks in your portfolio, giving you suggested courses of actions to take

Caveats

This application is not actually a trading account substitute. As a result everything done on this site is purely for study and simlutation purposes, no money is actually exchanged, no stocks actually purchased. To actually trade stocks one would need to follow the appropriate procedure depending on the area they wish to trade from.
